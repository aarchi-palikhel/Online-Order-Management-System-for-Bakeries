About Live Bakery

Serving Kamalbinayak, Bhaktapur with quality baked goods since 2025

Our Story

Established in the heart of Kamalbinayak, Live Bakery was born from a passion for creating exceptional baked goods that bring people together. Our founder envisioned a neighborhood bakery that would serve as a gathering place for the community, providing fresh, quality products for daily enjoyment and special celebrations.

Located in Kamalbinayak, Bhaktapur, we take pride in being part of this vibrant community. Our bakery has become a trusted source for families, students, and professionals seeking quality baked goods made with care and attention to detail.

"Our mission is simple: to provide the local community with fresh, delicious bakery products that make every day special and every celebration memorable."

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Quality Assurance

We use only the finest ingredients and maintain strict quality standards in every product we bake, ensuring consistency and excellence.

Freshness First

Every item is baked fresh daily. We never use day-old products, guaranteeing the best taste and texture for our customers.

Community Focus

We're deeply committed to the Kamalbinayak community, supporting local events and building relationships with our neighbors and customers.
