Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you deliver outside Kamalbinayak?

Yes! We deliver across Bhaktapur and surrounding areas. Delivery charges may vary based on location. Contact us for specific delivery areas.

Can I order custom cakes for special occasions?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes for birthdays, anniversaries, and celebrations. Please contact us at least 3 days in advance for custom orders to ensure availability.

Do you accept bulk orders for events?

Yes, we cater to events and parties. For bulk orders, we recommend placing your order at least one week in advance. Contact us directly for bulk order pricing and options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and mobile banking (eSewa). All online payments are secure and encrypted for your safety.
